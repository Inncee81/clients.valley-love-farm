About us

Valley Love Farm was started to bring high quality produce to local customers. We work hard to maintain sustainable and environmentally friendly practices by allowing our chickens to range free. This provides benefits to the environment and produces a healthier chicken, which in turn provides a healtiher you!

Community Supported Agriculture (CSA) is the perfect way for a customer, who cares about local food, to ally with a farmer and get the best they have to offer.
